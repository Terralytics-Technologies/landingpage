Three High-Value Decision Contexts

Each application below is framed around a concrete decision problem, a clear operational outcome, and a pathway to scalable deployment.

Arctic Defence Infrastructure

Decision Problem

How exposed are critical northern assets to climate risk, and where should resilience investments be prioritized?

What Terralytics Enables
  • Assess climate and flood risk to defence infrastructure such as airstrips, radar, and logistics assets
  • Quantify expected damage and operational disruption
  • Evaluate the impact of protective measures and infrastructure upgrades
  • Prioritize investments based on mission continuity and risk reduction
Why It Matters

Climate risk directly affects operational readiness and the reliability of northern infrastructure.

Housing Finance and Mortgage Risk

Decision Problem

How does climate risk affect mortgage portfolios, lending decisions, and financial stability?

What Terralytics Enables
  • Quantify property-level exposure across mortgage portfolios
  • Translate climate risk into expected financial impact at the property and portfolio level
  • Support risk-based lending, pricing, and capital allocation
  • Enable scenario analysis under changing climate conditions
Why It Matters

Financial institutions increasingly require defensible, property-level risk intelligence to manage exposure and meet regulatory expectations.

Municipal Infrastructure Planning

Decision Problem

Where should municipalities prioritize investments to reduce climate risk and protect communities?

What Terralytics Enables
  • Identify high-risk infrastructure and exposed communities
  • Evaluate the impact of flood mitigation options
  • Quantify avoided damage and the financial return on resilience investments
  • Support transparent, defensible infrastructure prioritization
Why It Matters

Municipalities face growing pressure to allocate limited resources toward the highest-impact resilience investments.
